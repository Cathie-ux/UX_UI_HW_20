sporTTeam

Designed a responsive website for a nonprofit animal rescue, education and advocacy group.

Team of 3 | My Role: UX/UI Designer, Project Manager

Participants who want to play sports have abandoned them because they don’t have a companion or team in their vicinity to play with them. Users need to find a community of friends to make the same sporting events with.

Our research helped us narrow down our problem statement, so we came up with our final design question:
How might we help people who want to do play their favorite sport(s) but don’t have friends who play the same sport and need to find a partner to play with.

People stopped participating in organized sports because of the lack of company and the limited number of friends practicing the same sport. Some became depressed when they stopped doing sports. With busy and limited schedules, many people lack the time to meet new people.

We found that 50% of people gave up sports because they didn’t have a partner to play with. The struggles that users were going through regarding team sports, such as lack of friends who played the same sports, time availability and matching skills that actually wanted to play those sports with them.

Create a sports community to connect people to play sports together so they can get active and feel better. Provide accessibility to connect with others who share the same athletic interests. Users will be able to play their favorite sports, whenever they want because they no longer require a small circle of knowledge to do so. Meet new participants to play at their sporting level, at any time or space and enable users to organize hours of play.
